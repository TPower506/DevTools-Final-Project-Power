Japan’s economic recovery in 2020 was relatively strong. By Q4, real GDP was down just 1.2% from a year earlier, thanks to a rebound in consumer spending and ample foreign demand.

Japan’s economy returned to growth at the end of 2021, fueled by the power of consumer spending that is now threatened by the latest omicron wave. and by Jan 2022 It was Skyrocketing.
